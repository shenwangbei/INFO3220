Wangbei Shen's Pool  Game:

===========================================================================================
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GOAL:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- The goal of the game is simple; Player can use cue to hit ball to pocket.
  Composite ball can break to subball due to strength smaller than energy of collision

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SHOOTING:
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- If you left-click and the white ball stops moving, set the mouse position to the end of the cue
- If the mouse is dragged and the white ball stops moving, set the mouse position to the end of the cue and display label
- The tag display the speed and angle
